Frequently Asked Questions

What types of animals do you rescue?

We rescue a wide variety of wildlife including raccoons, squirrels, foxes, birds, and more. We also assist in the rescue and rehabilitation of reptiles, deer, pigs, and horses.

How can I help Wild Rescues?

You can help by donating, volunteering, or even becoming a partner with us. We also appreciate the support of local businesses and individuals who can offer their time and resources.

Do you accept injured animals directly?

Yes, we do accept injured, sick, or orphaned wildlife. If you find an animal in need, please contact us immediately. We may provide transportation or give instructions on how to safely help the animal.

What happens to animals after they are rehabilitated?

Once an animal has been rehabilitated and is fit to survive in the wild, we release them back to their natural habitat. We aim to release every animal in the same area where they were found, ensuring a smooth transition back into the wild.
